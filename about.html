About TrustPay KE

Kenya's trusted peer-to-peer escrow and mediation platform. Secure your online transactions with confidence.

Who We Are

TrustPay KE is Kenya's leading escrow service provider, dedicated to making online transactions safe, transparent, and secure for everyone. We act as a trusted neutral third party between buyers and sellers, ensuring that funds are protected until both parties are satisfied with the transaction.

Founded with the mission to eliminate fraud in online trading, TrustPay KE has helped thousands of Kenyans trade safely across various platforms including Jiji, Facebook Marketplace, Instagram shops, and direct peer-to-peer transactions.

How It Works

Our escrow process is simple and secure:

  1. Agreement: Buyer and seller agree on terms and price
  2. Deposit: Buyer deposits funds to TrustPay KE via M-PESA
  3. Confirmation: We verify payment and confirm escrow is active
  4. Delivery: Seller delivers goods or completes services
  5. Release: Buyer confirms satisfaction; funds released to seller
