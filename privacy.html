Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

TrustPay KE ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our escrow services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, physical address
  • Account Information: Username, password, profile information
  • Transaction Information: Payment details, transaction history, escrow order details
  • Communication Data: Messages between parties, customer support communications
  • Identity Verification: National ID number, business registration documents when required

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (IP address, browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent on site)
  • Location data (general location based on IP)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Escrow Services: Process transactions, hold funds, facilitate payments between buyers and sellers
  • Account Management: Create and manage your account, verify identity
  • Customer Support: Respond to inquiries, resolve disputes, provide assistance
  • Marketing: Send promotional materials (with consent)
  • Legal Compliance: Prevent fraud, comply with legal obligations
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve our services

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Transaction Parties: Buyers and sellers involved in escrow transactions
  • Service Providers: Payment processors, technology partners
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to prevent fraud
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, sale, or acquisition

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure data storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Employee training on data protection

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide escrow services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

Transaction records are retained for at least 7 years as required by financial regulations.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Restriction: Request restriction of data processing
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to processing for direct marketing

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site traffic
  • Improve our services

You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect functionality.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Kenya Data Protection Act Compliance

TrustPay KE is committed to compliance with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019 (the "Act"), which establishes the framework for data protection in Kenya. As a data controller and processor, we ensure:

  • Lawful Processing: All personal data is processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently in accordance with Section 26 of the Act.
  • Purpose Limitation: Data is collected for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes as outlined in this policy.
  • Data Minimisation: We only collect data that is adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary.
  • Accuracy: We take reasonable steps to ensure personal data is accurate and kept up to date.
  • Storage Limitation: Data is retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.
  • Integrity and Confidentiality: Appropriate technical and organisational measures protect personal data.

Under the Act, you have the following additional rights:

  • Right to be informed of the identity and contact details of the data controller
  • Right to be informed of the purposes of collection and processing
  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to have inaccurate personal data rectified
  • Right to have your personal data erased (the "right to be forgotten")

12. General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) Compliance

Although TrustPay KE primarily serves users in Kenya, we are committed to GDPR principles for users who may interact with our services from European Union countries or who are EU citizens. We endeavour to apply GDPR-equivalent protections regardless of user location.

Our GDPR-aligned practices include:

  • Lawful Basis: We identify a lawful basis for each processing activity (consent, contract, legitimate interest, or legal obligation).
  • Data Protection Impact Assessments: We conduct assessments for high-risk processing activities.
  • Breach Notification: We have procedures to notify relevant authorities within 72 hours of discovering a data breach.
  • Data Protection Officer: While not legally required, we have designated staff responsible for data protection matters.

14. M-PESA and Mobile Money Data Processing

TrustPay KE integrates with Safaricom's M-PESA service to facilitate payments. When you use M-PESA through our platform:

  • Transaction data is processed by both TrustPay KE and Safaricom in accordance with their respective privacy policies
  • M-PESA transaction records are maintained by Safaricom according to their data retention policies
  • TrustPay KE receives only transaction confirmation data necessary to process escrow releases
  • Users should also review Safaricom's M-PESA Privacy Policy

15. Data Transfers and Cross-Border Processing

Some of the third-party services we use (including cloud infrastructure providers and analytics tools) may involve data processing outside of Kenya. When personal data is transferred internationally:

  • We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as standard contractual clauses
  • We verify that the recipient country provides adequate data protection
  • We limit the data transferred to only what is necessary for the specific purpose

16. Automated Decision-Making and Profiling

TrustPay KE may use automated systems for:

  • Fraud detection and prevention
  • Risk assessment for transactions
  • User verification processes
  • Personalised content and recommendations

Users have the right to request human review of automated decisions that significantly affect them. To exercise this right, please contact our support team.
