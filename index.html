Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TrustPay KE escrow services.

Who holds the money?

The Admin account safely holds funds until delivery is confirmed. We act as a neutral third party.

Can I be scammed?

Payments go only to the Admin's official account (Paybill 542542). Never pay directly to unknown parties.

How fast is payment released?

Payments are released immediately after buyer confirmation. Disputes are mediated within 24-48 hours.

What if seller fails to deliver?

Buyer can raise a dispute. Our admin team will investigate and refund if necessary after reviewing evidence.

Who can use TrustPay KE?

Anyone in Kenya involved in peer-to-peer trade - freelancers, online sellers, marketplace buyers, and more.
