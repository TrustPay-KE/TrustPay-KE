Our Refund Policy

TrustPay provides full buyer protection. If a transaction is cancelled before delivery, you receive a complete refund minus the service fee.

100% Protected

If you cancel before delivery, you receive a FULL refund minus the service fee.

The service fee covers payment processing costs and is non-refundable.

How Refunds Work

The Refund Process:

1

Transaction Cancelled

Either buyer or seller cancels before the goods are delivered or service is completed

2

Request Submitted

Buyer submits a cancellation request through our platform or via WhatsApp

3

Verification

We verify that goods/services have not been delivered or completed

4

Refund Processed

Full purchase amount is refunded to buyer within 24 hours

Fee Breakdown

What You Pay

Transaction Amount + Service Fee = Total Payment

What You Get Back (on cancellation)

Full Transaction Amount (minus the service fee)

Example Calculation

Transaction Amount: KES 10,000
Service Fee (1.5%): KES 150
Total Paid: KES 10,150

If cancelled before delivery:
Refund: KES 10,000
Non-refundable: KES 150 (service fee)

Important Notes

Service fees are non-refundable. This is because the fee covers payment processing costs incurred regardless of transaction outcome. Once funds have been released to the seller after buyer confirmation, refunds are no longer available.

When Refunds Are Available

Full Refund (minus service fee):

• Transaction cancelled before goods are shipped or service begins
• Seller fails to deliver within agreed timeframe
• Digital goods not received within 24 hours
• Goods received are significantly different from description
• Service not delivered as agreed

Partial or No Refund:

• Buyer has already confirmed satisfaction and funds released
• Goods delivered match description exactly
• Service completed as agreed
• Dispute ruled in seller's favor

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a refund take?

Refunds are typically processed within 24 hours of verification. M-PESA refunds may take 1-2 business days to reflect in your account.

Can I get a refund after confirming receipt?

No. Once you confirm satisfaction and funds are released to the seller, the transaction is complete. Always verify goods/services before confirming.

What if I paid extra for express delivery?

The full purchase amount is refunded minus the service fee. Any additional fees for expedited services are also refunded.

What if the seller agrees to cancel but won't respond?

Contact our support team. If the holding period has expired without delivery confirmation, we will process the refund automatically.
