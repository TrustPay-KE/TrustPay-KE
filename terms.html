2. Description of Service

TrustPay KE provides a secure escrow service that:

  • Holds funds from buyers during transactions
  • Releases funds to sellers upon buyer confirmation
  • Provides dispute resolution services
  • Facilitates M-PESA transactions in Kenya

3. Escrow Process

3.1 Order Creation

To create an escrow order:

  • Buyer initiates the order and provides transaction details
  • Buyer makes payment via M-PESA to TrustPay KE
  • TrustPay KE verifies payment receipt
  • Buyer and seller are notified of confirmed escrow

3.2 Fund Release

Funds are released when:

  • Buyer confirms receipt of goods/services
  • Buyer is satisfied with the transaction
  • No dispute has been raised

3.3 Dispute Resolution

If a dispute arises:

  • Either party can raise a dispute within 14 days
  • Both parties must provide evidence
  • TrustPay KE will mediate and make a final decision
  • Decision is binding on both parties

4. Fees and Payments

Our Fee Structure:
