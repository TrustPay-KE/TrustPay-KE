M-PESA Security Tips 2026

Complete guide to keeping your M-PESA account secure. Protect your money from fraudsters with these essential tips.

M-PESA is the backbone of Kenya's digital economy, but it's also a prime target for fraudsters. In 2026, scammers are more sophisticated than ever. This guide covers everything you need to know to keep your M-PESA money safe.

Essential M-PESA Security Tips

1. Protect Your PIN Like Your Life

  • Never share your PIN - Not with family, friends, or anyone claiming to be from Safaricom
  • Use a strong PIN - Avoid obvious numbers like 0000, 1234, or your birthday
  • Change regularly - Update your PIN every few months
  • Don't write it down - Never store your PIN in your phone or wallet
  • Cover when entering - Always shield the keypad when entering your PIN

2. Enable Security Features

  • Two-factor authentication - Enable for mySafaricom app
  • Biometric login - Use fingerprint or face ID on your phone
  • Transaction limits - Set daily limits on your account
  • Register for M-PESA statements - Monitor your account regularly
  • Enable SIM swap protection - Contact Safaricom to prevent unauthorized SIM swaps

3. Recognize Common Scams

  • Fake M-PESA SMS - Messages claiming you've received money
  • Reversal scams - Callers claiming to reverse a transaction
  • Win alerts - Messages saying you've won a prize
  • Customer service impersonators - Fake support calls
  • SIM swap fraud - Criminals transferring your number

4. Safe Transaction Practices

  • Verify recipient - Always double-check the phone number before sending
  • Confirm amounts - Re-enter the amount before confirming
  • Use trusted networks - Avoid public WiFi for M-PESA transactions
  • Keep your phone secure - Use screen locks and avoid sharing your phone
  • Update your app - Keep mySafaricom app updated

What Safaricom Will NEVER Do

Important Warnings

Safaricom staff will NEVER:

  • Ask for your M-PESA PIN over phone or SMS
  • Ask for your OTP (One Time Password)
  • Ask you to dial codes to "verify" or "secure" your account
  • Send you links to update your details
  • Ask you to transfer money to a "safe account"
  • Call to ask for personal details to "upgrade" your account

What To Do If Compromised

  • Act immediately - Time is critical
  • Call Safaricom - 100 (from M-PESA) or 0722 000 000
  • Freeze your M-PESA - Request immediate freeze
  • Change all passwords - Banking apps, social media, email
  • File police report - Cybercrimes unit
  • Check for unauthorized transactions - Review your statement

Advanced Security Measures

  • Use a dedicated phone for banking - Keep it separate from your main phone
  • Install security software - Antivirus on your phone
  • Monitor accounts daily - Check M-PESA statements regularly
  • Set up transaction alerts - Get SMS for every transaction
  • Be careful with phone repairs - Unknown technicians could install spyware
  • Avoid loan apps - Some compromise your phone's security

Emergency Contacts

  • Safaricom Customer Care: 100 (free from M-PESA)
  • Safaricom Fraud Hotline: 0722 000 000
  • Police Cybercrimes: 020 356 3518
  • TrustPay KE: 0797 347 508
