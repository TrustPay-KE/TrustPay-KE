How to Verify Business Licenses in Kenya Online

Before doing business with any company in Kenya, verification is essential. Here's how to check if a business is legitimately registered.

Why Verify Business Registration?

  • Avoid fraudulent businesses
  • Ensure you're dealing with legitimate entities
  • Protect your money and personal information
  • Meet due diligence requirements

How to Verify a Business in Kenya

1. e-Citizen Portal

Kenya's e-Citizen platform allows you to search for registered businesses:

  • Visit ecitizen.go.ke
  • Navigate to Business Registration
  • Use the business name search function
  • View registration status and details

2. Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA)

Verify a business has a valid KRA PIN:

  • Visit kra.go.ilg.in or use iTax portal
  • Search for the business name
  • Verify tax compliance status

3. County Government Licenses

For operating licenses, check with the relevant county:

  • Visit your county's business licensing portal
  • Request business permit verification
  • Check for valid single business permit

What Information to Verify

  • Business registration number
  • Registered business name
  • Physical address
  • Directors/Proprietors
  • Registration date
  • KRA PIN validity

Red Flags

  • Business not found in official databases
  • Registration recently obtained
  • Address doesn't exist or is a residential area
  • Refuses to provide registration details

Safe Business Practices

Always verify businesses before making large payments. Use escrow services for transactions with new vendors to protect yourself.
