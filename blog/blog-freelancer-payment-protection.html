Freelancer Payment Protection: Never Work for Free Again

How Kenyan freelancers can ensure they get paid for their work using escrow services.

As a freelancer in Kenya, you've probably experienced the frustration of completing work only to have a client ghost you without payment. It's a pervasive problem that affects thousands of Kenyan freelancers.

The good news? Escrow services like TrustPay KE can protect you from working for free.

Why Freelancers Need Escrow

Traditional payment methods don't protect freelancers. When a client says they'll pay "after the work is done," they're essentially asking you to work on credit with no guarantee of repayment.

💡 The Solution

With escrow, the client deposits funds before you start working. The money is held safely and only released to you once you deliver the work and they confirm satisfaction.

How Escrow Protects Freelancers

  • Guaranteed Payment: Funds are secured before you start
  • Professional Image: Clients take you more seriously
  • Dispute Resolution: If issues arise, we mediate fairly
  • Peace of Mind: Focus on quality work, not chasing payments

Best Practices for Freelancers

  • Always suggest escrow for new clients
  • Clearly define deliverables before starting
  • Keep records of all communication
  • Use escrow for projects over KES 5,000
