M-Pesa Fraud Prevention: Complete 2026 Guide

Latest M-Pesa fraud tactics and how to protect yourself from scammers in Kenya.

M-Pesa is incredibly convenient, but scammers are always finding new ways to steal. Stay ahead with our 2026 fraud prevention guide.

📞 Call Scams

Scammers call claiming to be Safaricom, asking for your PIN or OTP. Safaricom NEVER asks for your PIN.

💬 WhatsApp Scams

Messages claiming you've won prizes or need to "verify" your account.

🔗 Fake Links

Links sent via SMS or WhatsApp that lead to fake M-Pesa pages.

👤 Impersonation

Scammers pretending to be family members needing emergency money.

How to Protect Yourself

  • Never share your PIN: Not with anyone, ever
  • Enable transaction notifications: Know immediately when money moves
  • Use escrow for large transactions: TrustPay KE protects both parties
  • Verify callers: Ask questions only real family would know
  • Check URLs carefully: Official Safaricom URLs end in .ke

✅ Safety Checklist

Never share your PIN • Enable M-Pesa alerts • Use escrow for strangers • Report suspicious calls to Safaricom
