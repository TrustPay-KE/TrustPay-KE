Safe M-Pesa Payments: Tips Every Kenyan Should Know

Practical tips to keep your M-Pesa transactions safe and secure every day.

M-Pesa is Kenya's mobile money leader, but with convenience comes risk. Here's how to stay safe.

🔐 Enable PIN Protection

Always use a strong M-Pesa PIN and never share it with anyone. Safaricom will NEVER ask for your PIN.

📱 Keep Your Phone Secure

Use phone locks (PIN, fingerprint, face ID) to protect your M-Pesa apps and mobile banking.

⚠️ Beware of Scam Calls

Scammers call pretending to be Safaricom staff. Never give your PIN or OTPs to anyone.

💰 Use Escrow for Large Transactions

For amounts over KES 10,000, use TrustPay KE escrow to protect both buyer and seller.

Quick Safety Checklist

  • ✅ Never share your M-Pesa PIN
  • ✅ Enable transaction notifications
  • ✅ Check your balance regularly
  • ✅ Use escrow for unfamiliar sellers
  • ✅ Report suspicious calls to Safaricom
