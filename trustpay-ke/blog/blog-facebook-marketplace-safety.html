Facebook Marketplace Safety: Buy and Sell Safely in Kenya

Essential tips for staying safe when buying or selling on Facebook Marketplace in Kenya.

Facebook Marketplace has become one of Kenya's most popular platforms for buying and selling. But with its popularity comes risk. Here's how to stay safe.

Red Flags to Watch For

  • Posts with stock photos instead of real images
  • Prices too good to be true
  • Sellers who refuse to meet in person
  • Requests to pay before seeing the item
  • Sellers with no profile history

🛡️ Use Escrow

For valuable items, always use TrustPay KE escrow. The buyer deposits funds, and you receive payment only after they confirm receipt.

Safe Buying Tips

  • Always meet in public places
  • Inspect items before paying
  • Test electronics on the spot
  • Bring a friend when possible
  • Trust your instincts

Safe Selling Tips

  • Meet buyers in safe locations
  • Don't share personal banking details
  • Use escrow for high-value items
  • Keep copies of messages
