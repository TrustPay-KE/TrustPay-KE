Common Questions

Who pays the service fee?

The buyer pays the service fee. It's added to the transaction amount so you know exactly how much to pay upfront.

Are fees refundable?

Service fees cover payment processing and platform protection costs. If you cancel before delivery, you get a refund MINUS the service fee. Fees are non-refundable once the transaction is complete.

Is there a minimum transaction?

No minimum! Transactions below KES 200 are completely FREE. You only pay fees for transactions over KES 200.

What payment methods are accepted?

We currently accept M-PESA payments only. This ensures fast, secure transactions for all Kenyan users.
