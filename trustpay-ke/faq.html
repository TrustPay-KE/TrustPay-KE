Getting Started

Escrow is a secure payment method where a trusted third party (TrustPay) holds funds until a transaction is complete.

Here's how it works:

  1. Buyer and seller agree on terms
  2. Buyer deposits money to TrustPay via M-PESA
  3. Seller delivers goods or completes the service
  4. Buyer confirms receipt and satisfaction
  5. TrustPay releases funds to seller (minus small fee)

This protects both parties from fraud - buyers don't lose money if goods aren't delivered, and sellers know payment is secured.

Payments are made through M-PESA, Kenya's most popular mobile money service.

Steps:

  1. Start your transaction on TrustPay
  2. You'll receive M-PESA payment instructions
  3. Go to M-PESA → Pay Bill → Business Number: 542542
  4. Account Number: 05306274246151
  5. Enter the total amount (including service fee)
  6. We'll confirm receipt within minutes

Direct M-PESA payments offer zero protection. If you pay upfront and the seller doesn't deliver, your money is gone forever.

With TrustPay, you get:

  • Funds held securely until you confirm receipt
  • Dispute resolution if something goes wrong
  • Professional mediation for fair outcomes
  • Protection against non-delivery scams
  • Verification window for physical goods

Fees are added to the buyer's payment. Service fees are non-refundable as they cover payment processing costs.

Before delivery: If you cancel a transaction BEFORE the seller delivers or completes work, you will receive a FULL refund MINUS the service fee.

After confirmation: Once you confirm satisfaction and funds are released to the seller, refunds are not available. This is why it's important to verify goods/services before confirming.

Note: Service fees cover payment processing and platform protection costs and are non-refundable.

Release time depends on transaction type:

  • Services: Immediate after buyer confirmation (or auto-release after 3-5 days if no response)
  • Digital Goods: 6-24 hour verification window
  • Physical Goods: 2-5 days after delivery confirmation for inspection
  • Custom: Timeline agreed by both parties before payment

You'll always see the expected release time BEFORE making payment.
